Tone down overstimulating or visually aggressive designs.

## Steps

1. Read `conventions/design/anti-patterns.md`
2. Identify visual noise in $ARGUMENTS:
   - Too many competing focal points
   - Excessive contrast (pure black on white, neon accents)
   - Too many animations or moving elements
   - Dense layouts with no breathing room
3. Apply calming adjustments:
   - Reduce contrast: use off-white backgrounds, tinted grays
   - Soften colors: desaturate by 10-20%, avoid pure hues
   - Slow animations: increase duration, use ease-out
   - Add whitespace: increase padding, section spacing
   - Reduce borders and shadows: use subtle dividers
4. Verify hierarchy survives:
   - Primary action still stands out
   - Navigation still scannable
   - Information hierarchy maintained

## Rule
Calm ≠ boring. Reduce volume without removing character.
